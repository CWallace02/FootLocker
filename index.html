Foot-Locker

Foot-Locker is a great place to shop for shoes, tops, bottoms, hats, bags and more.

  1. Foot Locker was founded in 1974.
  2. The founders are F.W. Woolworth and Santiago Lopez.
  3. Foot Locker's headquarter is located in New York, NY.
  4. The CEO is Richard Johnson.
  5. Key Foot Locker brands include Foot Locker, Lady Foot Locker Kid's Foot Locker, Champs Sports, and Eastbay.
